Our Story

Founded in New York City in 1995, Structural Solutions began as a boutique engineering firm with a passion for innovative structural design.

Over the past three decades, we've grown into a nationally recognized firm while maintaining our commitment to technical excellence and personalized service. Our portfolio now includes award-winning projects across commercial, residential, and infrastructure sectors.
